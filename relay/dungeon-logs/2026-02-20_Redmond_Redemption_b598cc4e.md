## Arc Narratif

Hook: A desperate mother contacts the runners, pleading for help. Her son, wrongly accused of corporate espionage, is being held in a heavily guarded Renraku facility in Redmond.
Fin: The runners extract the son, exposing Renraku's corrupt practices. They must then evade both Renraku security forces and a rival extraction team hired by a shadowy figure who wants the son silenced. Victory comes from successfully delivering him to a safe haven outside Renraku's reach, revealing the corporation's illegal experiments on augmented individuals to the public, and exposing the shadowy figure as a rival corp executive.

Etapes:
1. The Plea: The runners are contacted by Maria, the mother of the wrongfully accused. She provides background information on her son, Ben, and the Renraku facility.
2. Redmond Recon: The team must scout the Renraku facility in Redmond, gathering intel on security measures, patrol routes, and possible entry points. Glitch/Pixel can provide digital reconnaissance.
3. Breach and Clear: The runners execute their plan to infiltrate the Renraku facility, overcoming security measures and corporate guards. Nicolay's rigging skills are crucial here.
4. Extraction Point: The runners locate Ben and begin the extraction. They face resistance from Renraku security forces and discover evidence of illegal experimentation.
5. The Chase: The runners, along with Ben, must escape the facility and evade pursuit from Renraku security. They are also hunted by a rival team hired by a competing corporation who wants Ben silenced. They must reach a predetermined safe house.
